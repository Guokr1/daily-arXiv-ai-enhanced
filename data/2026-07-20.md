### [1] [Nuclotron internal target polarimeter for the measurements of the deuteron and proton beam polarization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15892)
*I. S. Volkov,V. P. Ladygin,A. V. Averyanov,E. V. Chernykh,D. Enache,Yu. V. Gurchin,A. Yu. Isupov,M. Janek,J. -T. Karachuk,D. O. Krivenkov,P. K. Kurilkin,A. N. Livanov,S. M. Piyadin,S. G. Reznikov,Ya. T. Skhomenko,A. A. Terekhin,A. V. Tishevsky,T. Uesaka,I. E. Vnukov*

Main category: physics.ins-det

TL;DR: 本文在Nuclotron/NICA加速器上测量了氘核（200-650 MeV/nucleon）和质子（500 MeV）束流的矢量极化度，利用塑料闪烁计数器探测系统，通过聚乙烯和碳靶上的散射不对称性确定极化值。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: NICA加速器复合体的自旋相关效应研究需要精确测量氘核和质子束的极化度，这是开展后续自旋物理实验的基础。

Method: 采用闪烁计数器探测系统在Nuclotron内靶上测量氘核束（200、500、550、650 MeV/nucleon）的矢量极化；将氘核视为弱束缚的质子-中子系统，通过聚乙烯和碳靶上质子-氘核散射不对称性确定极化；同时测量了首次加速至500 MeV的极化质子束。

Result: 成功获得了多个能量点下氘核束的矢量极化度测量结果，以及500 MeV极化质子束的极化测量结果。

Conclusion: 该工作为NICA自旋物理项目提供了关键的束流极化诊断手段，验证了极化束加速与测量系统的可行性。

Abstract: Studies of spin-dependent effects at the Nuclotron/NICA accelerator complex at JINR require precise measurements of the deuteron and proton beam polarization. The vector polarization of the deuteron beam was measured at the energies of 200, 500, 550, and 650 MeV/nucleon by a detection system of scintillation counters placed at the Nuclotron internal target. Considering the deuteron beam as a beam of weakly bound protons and neutrons, the asymmetries of scattering of protons from deuterons on polyethylene and carbon targets were determined. The polarization of the polarized proton beam accelerated for the first time at the Nuclotron up to 500 MeV was also measured.

### [2] [Precision positioning in free-space optical communication systems via PID control tuned by RL](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15910)
*K. Prikhodko,S. Kuznetsov,S. Vorobey,A. Katanskiy,V. Balakirev,A. Reutov*

Main category: physics.ins-det

TL;DR: 本文研究利用深度确定性策略梯度（DDPG）强化学习算法，为自由空间光通信系统中的光纤定位器自动调优级联PID控制器参数。经过5000次训练，最佳参数集在动态目标跟踪任务中将径向定位误差范围降低31%，但在静态目标场景下未见改善。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 自由空间光通信系统中，光学元件需精确定位以维持光束对准，而传统手动调参方式耗时且难以达到最优性能，因此需要自动化的PID调优方法。

Method: 使用DDPG强化学习智能体，通过与物理实验台交互（远程UDP通信，相距约200km），对级联位置-速度PID控制器的六个系数进行在线调优，训练5000个回合后选出两组固定参数。

Result: 动态伪随机目标轨迹下，最佳RL调优参数将径向定位误差范围从119降至82（降幅31%），标准差从15降至12；但在静态零点目标下，RL调优未改善误差范围。

Conclusion: DDPG在动态定位任务的实验级PID调优中展现出潜力，但需发展多工况联合优化策略，以在不同运行条件下保持一致的性能表现。

Abstract: Accurate positioning of optical components is essential for maintaining beam alignment in free-space optical (FSO) communication systems. This work investigates reinforcement-learning-assisted tuning of cascaded position and velocity PID controllers for an optical deflector that moves the end of an optical fiber in the focal plane of an optical system. A Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient (DDPG) agent adjusts six PID coefficients through interaction with a physical experimental stand. The stand supports target-coordinate updates of up to $12$ kHz, while the agent and the controlled device are located approximately $200$ km apart and exchange data over UDP. After $5000$ training sessions, two fixed coefficient sets are selected and compared with a manually tuned baseline. For a pseudo-random target trajectory, the best RL-tuned set reduces the range of the radial positioning error from $119$ to $82$, corresponding to a $31\%$ reduction, and decreases its standard deviation from $15$ to $12$. For a constant zero target, the RL-tuned sets do not improve the radial error range. The results demonstrate the potential of DDPG for experimental PID tuning in dynamic positioning tasks and indicate the need for multi-regime optimization to achieve consistent performance under different operating conditions.
