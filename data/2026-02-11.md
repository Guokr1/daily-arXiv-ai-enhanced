The performance significantly exceeds the design requirement of $\leq 3\%/\sqrt{E(\mathrm{GeV})}\oplus1\%$. The results establish the feasibility of the proposed high-granularity crystal calorimeter concept and point to a promising pathway toward the precision calorimetry required for future high-energy electron-positron collider experiments.
