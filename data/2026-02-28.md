### [1] [Testing the Weak Gravity Conjecture via Gravitational Lensing, Black Hole Shadows, and Barrow Thermodynamics in F(R)-Euler-Heisenberg (A)dS Black Holes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22930)
*Saeed Noori Gashti,Izzet Sakalli,Erdem Sucu,Mohammad Reza Alipour,Ankit Anand,Mohammad Ali S Afshar,Behnam Pourhassan,Jafar Sadeghi*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 研究F(R)-Euler-Heisenberg黑洞中弱引力猜想与弱宇宙监督猜想的相互作用，建立了普适的熵-极值关系，验证了弱引力猜想的兼容性，并分析了黑洞热力学相变。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索在F(R)修正引力理论和Euler-Heisenberg非线性电动力学背景下，弱引力猜想(WGC)与弱宇宙监督猜想(WCCC)之间的相互作用关系，特别是在Anti-de Sitter和de Sitter时空中的表现。

Method: 通过建立熵-极值关系提供热力学证据；从测地线和拓扑角度进行光子球分析；研究强/弱偏折极限下的引力透镜效应；构建各向同性吸积下的黑洞阴影图像；在Barrow熵框架下分析范德瓦尔斯型相变和焦耳-汤姆逊膨胀。

Result: 建立了与f_{R_0}和R_0无关的普适熵-极值关系，为WGC提供了热力学证据；光子球分析确认WGC与WCCC同时兼容；发现光子球半径与宇宙学背景无关，但de Sitter时空的临界碰撞参数几乎翻倍；在Barrow熵框架下发现了范德瓦尔斯型相变，并确定小黑洞相是可通过等焓冷却访问的WGC兼容热力学区域。

Conclusion: F(R)-Euler-Heisenberg黑洞中弱引力猜想与弱宇宙监督猜想是兼容的，Euler-Heisenberg耦合在裸奇点区域恢复了光子球，黑洞热力学相变分析进一步支持了弱引力猜想的有效性。

Abstract: We investigate the interplay of the Weak Gravity Conjecture (WGC) and the Weak Cosmic Censorship Conjecture (WCCC) in $F(R)$-Euler-Heisenberg black holes in Anti-de Sitter and de Sitter backgrounds. The solution is characterized by the electric charge $q$, the $F(R)$ deviation $f_{R_0}$, the Euler--Heisenberg coupling $λ$, and the constant scalar curvature $R_0$. We establish a universal entropy--extremality relation that provides thermodynamic evidence for the WGC independently of $f_{R_0}$ and $R_0$. Photon sphere analysis from both geodesic and topological perspectives confirms the simultaneous compatibility of the WGC and WCCC, with the Euler--Heisenberg coupling restoring photon spheres in the naked singularity regime. Gravitational lensing in the strong- and weak-deflection limits reveals that the photon sphere radius is independent of the cosmological background while the critical impact parameter nearly doubles in de Sitter. Black hole shadow images under isotropic accretion are constructed. Within the Barrow entropy framework, we uncover van der Waals-type phase transitions and analyze Joule-Thomson expansion, identifying the small black hole phase as the WGC-compatible thermodynamic regime accessible via isenthalpic cooling.

### [2] [Charged scalar and Dirac perturbations on a global monopole Reissner-Nordström-de Sitter black hole: quasinormal modes and strong cosmic censorship](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23083)
*Peiyang Li,Mengjie Wang,Jiliang Jing*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 研究带全局单极子的Reissner-Nordström-de Sitter黑洞周围带电标量场和Dirac场的扰动，使用Heun函数方法求解准正规模谱，分析全局单极子对强宇宙监督猜想的影响。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究带全局单极子的渐近de Sitter黑洞与扰动场的相互作用，特别是全局单极子对准正规模谱和强宇宙监督猜想的影响。

Method: 首先推导带电标量场和Dirac场在Reissner-Nordström-de Sitter黑洞加全局单极子背景下的运动方程，将其统一化为Teukolsky方程。利用Teukolsky方程在渐近de Sitter时空可映射到Heun方程的特性，采用Heun函数方法求解准正规模谱，包括光子球模、de Sitter模和近极端模。

Result: 分析了两种场所有三种类型模的谱，确定了全局单极子的影响。在近极端区域发现：对于标量扰动，全局单极子不影响强宇宙监督猜想；对于Dirac扰动，全局单极子增强了强宇宙监督猜想的违反。全局单极子通过改变修正的多极子数来影响谱和强宇宙监督。

Conclusion: Heun函数方法是研究渐近de Sitter黑洞与扰动场相互作用的有效且稳健的方法。全局单极子对强宇宙监督猜想的影响取决于扰动场的类型：标量场不受影响，Dirac场违反增强。

Abstract: We study perturbations of charged scalar and Dirac fields around Reissner-Nordström-de Sitter black holes with a global monopole. To this end, we first derive the equations of motion for both fields on the aforementioned background; these equations are then reformulated uniformly into the Teukolsky equation. Since the Teukolsky equation in asymptotically de Sitter spacetimes can be mapped into the Heun equation, we are able to solve quasinormal spectra by employing the Heun function method, not only for photon sphere modes but also for de Sitter and near-extremal modes. We analyze the spectra of all three types for both fields and, in particular, ascertain the effects of the global monopole. In the near-extremal regime, we find that the presence of a global monopole, on the one hand, leaves the strong cosmic censorship conjecture unaffected for scalar perturbations, while on the other hand, it enhance the violation of strong cosmic censorship for Dirac perturbations. Furthermore, we identify that the impact of the global monopole on both the spectra and the strong cosmic censorship is achieved by a shift in the modified multipole number. Our work demonstrates that the Heun function method is an efficient and robust approach for exploring the interaction between asymptotically de Sitter black holes and perturbing fields.

### [3] [Spherically Symmetric Gravity on a Graph I: Theoretical Foundations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23308)
*Klaus Liegener,Saeed Rastgoo,Jorden Roberts*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 本文是系列研究的第一篇，探讨环量子引力有效动力学中球对称解的构建，为后续研究k=1宇宙学和黑洞解奠定基础。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 在环量子引力有效动力学框架下，系统研究球对称解的理论基础，为后续具体物理应用（如宇宙学和黑洞）提供数学工具和计算框架。

Method: 采用适应球对称性的晶格选择，使其在对称变换下保持不变；识别对称性约束下的动力学子空间；在该子空间上显式计算辛结构和标量约束。

Result: 建立了球对称解的有效动力学计算框架，确定了可行的计算子空间，并完成了辛结构和标量约束的显式计算，为后续具体物理应用做好了准备。

Conclusion: 本文成功构建了环量子引力中球对称解的有效动力学基础框架，为后续研究k=1宇宙学和黑洞等具体物理问题奠定了坚实的数学基础。

Abstract: This manuscript is the first in a series of instalments that investigate spherically symmetric solutions within the effective dynamics program of Loop Quantum Gravity. The choice of lattice is adapted such that it remains invariant under a set of symmetry transformations maximally mapping spherical symmetry to the discrete setting. The conditions for symmetry restriction of the dynamics are investigated and a subspace is identified to make computations feasible. Afterwards symplectic structure and scalar constraint are explicitly computed on this subspace. This lays the groundwork to target several particular solutions, such $k=1$ cosmology and black holes, which will serve as the subjects of forthcoming follow-up papers.
